The Terrific Trio: QUEST‘s Research Team Presents in Montreal!

On May 31st, 2014 QUEST sent a delegation to The Industrial and Systems Engineering Research Conference in Montreal, Canada. Max Cooper, Kylie Goodell, and Dr. Jeffrey Herrmann joined over 16,000 attendees from industry, government, and academia to present their research. This was a terrific opportunity for QUEST to expand its brand and learn from leading professionals in their field, “The event was trés bien!”
