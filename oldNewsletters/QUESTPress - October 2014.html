QUEST Alumni Board Gets Regional!

To continue to strengthen the QUEST alumni network and increase involvement and interaction, the QAB has begun developing regional chapters.

We will pilot three regions this fall: the Washington D.C. Metro Area, Manhattan, and the San Francisco Bay Area. A minimum of one quarterly alumni event will take place in each of the pilot regions, so make sure to stay tuned!

WiseGuy.com - Unleash the Power of Peer Learning

Read about Jeremy Loya's journey from college to eventually develop and co-found the WiseGuy (www.wiseguy.com) platform with two co-founders, John Bafford and Corey Cines. The Beta launch will be this Fall semester, and they're hoping to become the next big thing in EdTech or just enjoy the journey!

WiseGuy.com is a university focused peer learning platform being launched at UMD this Fall that enables academic programs and student organizations to launch, manage, and scale their own online tutoring and mentoring programs.

WiseGuy is also hiring a Marketing / Community Manager! Shoot Jeremy (jloya@wiseguy.com) an email if you're interested!

Effective Presentations Workshop

On Monday September 8th, Dr. Armstrong was able to bring in Dr. Oliver Schlake to talk to QUEST students about developing effective presentations. The workshop was intimate but students got a lot out of their time.

For Q19 student David Kravitz the biggest takeaway was that "if your slides can function without you, you're not doing PowerPoint right. It is your job to bring the slides to life."

According to Q21 member Bobby Fitzgerald, "the best part of the workshop was the presentation transformation."
